Trézor Bridge® | Introducing the New Trézor App

Trezór Bridge is a lightweight software application that acts as a secure communication channel between your Trezór hardware wallet and your web browser. Essentially, it "bridges" the gap, ensuring your browser-based crypto interfaces (like Trezór Suite or other compatible platforms) can safely interact with your physical wallet.

Get Started

Why Do You Need Trezór Bridge?

  • ✔️ Secure Communication: It establishes a safe path between your device and online applications without exposing sensitive data.
  • ✔️ Cross-Browser Support: Instead of relying on individual browser extensions, Bridge works across Chrome, Firefox, and other popular browsers.
  • ✔️ Better Performance: It reduces connection errors and improves the overall interaction between the Trezór device and web-based services.
  • ✔️ Future-Proofing: As browsers phase out support for traditional plugins, Bridge ensures ongoing compatibility.
